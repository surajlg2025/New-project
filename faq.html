frequently asked questions

What is Sahifasal?

Sahifasal is a smart farming platform that helps farmers decide what and when to grow using climate and market data.

How does season prediction work?

We analyze weather forecast data and rainfall trends to estimate season duration and sowing windows.

Are market prices real?

Yes. Market prices are fetched from government agricultural market APIs.

Can this help increase profit?

Yes. By choosing crops based on demand and climate conditions, farmers can reduce risk and improve profitability.
